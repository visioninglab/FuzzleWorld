Felt Puzzle

A Giant Insect World puzzle
build it · take it apart · build it again

You will need:

  • Reddish-brown & pale-orange felt
  • Scissors
  • This instruction sheet and template
  • The brown felt mat for soil habitat (optional)

How to build it

  1. 1Trace each shape onto the reddish-brown felt.
  2. 2Cut out all 3 pieces carefully.
  3. 3Lay the brown mat down — that's your soil!
  4. 4Place the front body, pointed head at the top.
  5. 5Add the rear body below; curve them into an S.
  6. 6Lay the pale saddle over the join. Done!
⭐

Did you know?

  • No eyes, ears or bones — it breathes through its skin.
  • It eats its way through soil, recycling dead leaves.
  • Its tunnels let air and water into the ground.
  • Charles Darwin studied worms for 39 years!
  • Tiny bristles grip the soil so it can pull itself along.
