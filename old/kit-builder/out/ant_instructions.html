build it · take it apart · build it again

You will need:

  • Black felt or paper
  • Scissors
  • This instruction sheet and template
  • The brown felt mat for soil habitat (optional)

How to build it

  1. 1Trace each shape onto the black felt.
  2. 2Cut out all 11 pieces carefully.
  3. 3Lay the brown mat down — that's your soil!
  4. 4Place head, thorax and gaster in a line — leave a thin waist.
  5. 5Add 6 legs from the thorax, three each side.
  6. 6Add 2 bent antennae on the head. Done!
⭐

Did you know?

  • Ants live in huge colonies ruled by one big queen.
  • They "talk" with smells and little touches.
  • An ant can lift 50× its own body weight!
  • On warm days, winged ants fly off to start new nests.
  • Its 3 parts: head, thorax and gaster (the big rear).

The pieces

Find these colours in felt or paper

Black

Using the template, cut out:

Head ×1 — black
Thorax ×1 — black
Gaster ×1 — black (big rear)
Legs ×6 — black (bent in middle)
Antennae ×2 — black (bent)

That's 11 shapes in total!

Just one colour — the simplest kit in the set!
