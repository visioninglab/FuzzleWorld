Common Darter Dragonfly
Felt Puzzle

A Giant Insect World puzzle
build it · take it apart · build it again

You will need:

  • Orange, pale-cream & dark-brown felt
  • Scissors
  • This instruction sheet and template
  • The blue felt mat for pond habitat (optional)

How to build it

  1. 1Trace each shape onto the right felt colour.
  2. 2Cut out all 7 pieces carefully.
  3. 3Lay the blue mat down — that's your pond!
  4. 4Place the long orange body down the middle.
  5. 5Add 2 wings each side — spread them out wide.
  6. 6Add the 2 dark eyes at the top (the head). Done!
⭐

Did you know?

  • One of the last dragonflies still flying in autumn.
  • Its 4 wings beat separately — it can fly backwards.
  • Huge eyes see almost all the way around.
  • A fierce hunter, catching midges in mid-air.
  • It starts life underwater as a nymph.
⭐

The pieces

Find these colours in felt or paper

Orange · Pale cream · Dark brown

Using the template, cut out:

Body ×1 — orange
Wings ×4 — pale cream
Eyes ×2 — dark brown

That's 7 shapes in total!

Finished!

Your dragonfly is about 9 × 8 cm.
