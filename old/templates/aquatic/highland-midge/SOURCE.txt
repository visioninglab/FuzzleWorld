Note on Manchester distance:
   This is a Highland Midge — overwhelmingly a Scottish species — so there is
   NO good record anywhere near Manchester (53.4808, -2.2426). The nearest
   Creative-Commons, research-grade adult photo is on the Isle of Arran,
   roughly 300 km north-west of Manchester. Location is stated honestly in the
   kit ("The real thing" panel and back credits). Only 4 CC-licensed
   research-grade observations of this species exist worldwide on iNaturalist;
   this Arran macro (a single female biting skin, blood-filled abdomen,
   patterned wing, feathery antenna and dark eye all clearly visible) is by
   far the clearest adult image and was chosen for that reason.
