Notes: Nearest decent macro of an ADULT midge to Manchester. Forcipomyia are heavily
under-recorded; most nearby records are of the caterpillar-like larvae. This side-on
shot clearly shows the humped thorax, a single pale-veined wing over the back, the dark
eye and the fuzzy/feathery antennae — a good reference for a very small delicate fly.
