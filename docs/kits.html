FuzzleWorld Play Kits

Fuzzle = felt + puzzle. Print a pattern, cut the shapes from felt, and build a little British minibeast — then take it apart and build it again. Every kit is free to print.

1PrintA4, landscape, at 100% (actual size).
2TraceDraw each shape onto the right colour of felt.
3CutSnip out all the felt pieces.
4BuildLay the pieces on the mat to make your bug.
5RebuildTake it apart and build it again — no glue needed.

FuzzleWorld · part of Giant Insect World · 18 kits across three habitats.
